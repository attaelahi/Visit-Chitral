Description

Shandur Pass(Urdu: شندور) is a pass located in Chitral, District of Khyber Pakhtunkhawa . It is often called 'Roof of the World. During the annual 'Shandur Polo Festival,' there are polo matches played in Shandur Polo Ground on Shandur Top, between the teams of Chitral District and Gilgit-Baltistan Province. Approximately seven matches are played in the three days of the festival usually held on July 7th to 9th every year. The final match is played on July 9th between Teams A of Gilgit Baltistan and Chitral.
