Description

Madaklasht is a very small city located on the north side of Chitral, Pakistan. Until the eighteenth century, the community lived in a small village in this part of Pakistan. It is said that in the early 18th century, seven people immigrated to Chitral from Pakistan to produce firearms for their kingdom. They tried to establish their own base camps in various places but finally settled in Madakasht. The city had a population of 4,000 at this time, still speaking its native language. They avoid inter-caste marriages to preserve the purity of the race. This village on the north side of Chitral is one of the most stunning sights in Chitral, Pakistan.
